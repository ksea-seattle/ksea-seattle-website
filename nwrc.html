About NWRC 2019

Location: Sheraton Hotel Bellevue 100 112th Ave NE, Bellevue, WA 98004
Date: Saturday, November 2, 2019, 7:00 AM – 8:00 PM

The theme of NWRC 2019 is “Smart Science, Engineering and Technology”. Many technology advancements in 5G, Genomes, AI, Big Data, Cloud, Quantum, FinTech, etc. We would like to address challenges in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) to prepare for the era of the 4th industrial revolution. Specifically, we will discuss these issues in three tracks: smart science, smart engineering, and smart health, which were emphasized in UKC 2019. NWRC 2019 provides valuable opportunities for cooperation between industry, research, and academic communities among six local chapter members. Also, the conference will welcome new members and support all participants to build professional networks during the conference day through STREAM (STEM +Arts + Recreation) activities.
